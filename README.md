This is an all-in-one, quick and customizable feed for your media outlets, take control back and tune everything your news to the liking.

Made with Compose, [Room](https://developer.android.com/training/data-storage/room), [Hilt](https://developer.android.com/training/dependency-injection/hilt-android), [Coil](https://github.com/coil-kt/coil), [OkHttp](https://square.github.io/okhttp/), [Woodstox](https://github.com/fasterxml/woodstox) (via StAX), [MediaPipe Text Embedder](https://developers.google.com/edge/mediapipe/solutions/text/text_embedder) and [Compose Media Player](https://github.com/kdroidFilter/ComposeMediaPlayer).

MORE AT: [https://lucasangelo.dev/vox-populi/](https://lucasangelo.dev/vox-populi/)
